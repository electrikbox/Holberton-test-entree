About me

So, what can I say? My name is Olivier, I'm 44 years old and I'm French.

I did my first studies in graphic design, after that I worked in an advertising company for 3 years.
In parallel I was a musician, I did a lot of shows and studio sessions, and it's thanks to these studio sessions that I started to learn sound engineering, which I've been doing for more than 18 years now.

I'm also an administrator of a Rust (survival game) server since 4 years, and it's this "job" that made me interested in the world of computer development, so I started to learn by myself this field (as for the job of sound engineer, yes, I like to learn, I'm someone curious) with Python and C# languages with which I already created some plugins for Rust.

I don't know what else to tell you, I really had fun creating this little website, testing things, trying to find solutions to the problems that appeared. I know there is still a lot to learn, and as I said before, I am a curious person ;)

Here is one of the things that fascinates me, that makes me dizzy, that can sometimes be frightening : Astronomy !
Since I was a child, the universe and everything that happens in it, everything that is in this incredible immensity, fascinates me.
If you click on this round picture, you will see our whole solar system ;)
